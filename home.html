Azalea


The Azalea is popular for its spectacular flowers, which bloom in Spring, comes in many different colors, shapes, sizes and patterns. For an Azalea to flower abundantly you need to time your pruning and fertilizing.



japanese Maple


Japanese maples are the most dazzling trees for bonsai and are widely popular thanks to their brilliantly colored fall foliage. These compact trees offer a moderate growth habit that slows with age.



Satsuki-Azalea


Azalea bonsai trees thrive best in outdoor conditions. However, they can be kept indoors if cared for properly. During the winter, trees can be overwintered to protect from freezing and leaf yellowing.
